Here we used our "crème" adhesive-backed sueded polyethylene for the padding on the mount. The padding is also covering the drywall screw that is in the middle of the mount attaching the mount to the deck.

You can see one of the as-yet-unpainted-out arms of the bowl mount cradling the egg. We used the Small Bowl Mount for this egg but went ahead and shortened the arms even further. Less is more as long as the object is completely secure.
