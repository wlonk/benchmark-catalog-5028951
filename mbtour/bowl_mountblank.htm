Bowl MountBlank

The Bowl MountBlank is used to securely cradle round-bottomed artifacts. Shape the three fully annealed arms to fit the bottom of bowl, egg or other rounded object. Then secure the finished MountBlank to the deck with a standard drywall screw. Sometimes, for added stability and if the artifact will allow, we'll place one of our Weightbags inside the object.

The Bowl MountBlank comes in two sizes:

  Sizes

Small  (No. 22-291)

3/32" x 3/8" x 3 1/2" long arms

Large (No. 22-292)

3/32" x 1/2" x 4 1/2" long arms
